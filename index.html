Jujutsu Kaisen

About:

The story follows Yuji Itadori, a kind-hearted teenager with extraordinary physical abilities. After encountering a cursed object (a severed finger belonging to the powerful Curse Ryomen Sukuna), he becomes the host for Sukuna and is pulled into the dangerous world of Jujutsu Sorcerers—people who fight Curses, evil spirits born from human negative emotions. To prevent Sukuna’s power from falling into the wrong hands, Yuji joins the Tokyo Jujutsu High under the guidance of powerful sorcerer Satoru Gojo, aiming to collect and consume all of Sukuna’s fingers, knowing it will eventually lead to his own death. Where to start

  1. Start with the Anime – Season 1:
    2. Begin with Season 1 of the anime, which introduces Yuji Itadori, the world of Curses, and the Tokyo Jujutsu High. It's action-packed, emotional, and sets the foundation for everything that follows.

  2. Watch Jujutsu Kaisen 0 (Movie):
    3. After Season 1, watch Jujutsu Kaisen 0, a prequel that focuses on Yuta Okkotsu. It deepens the lore and introduces major characters that return later in the story.

  3. Continue with Season 2:
    4. Next, watch Season 2, which includes Gojo’s Past Arc and the explosive Shibuya Incident Arc—one of the most intense arcs in modern anime.

  4. Manga Readers:
    5. After season 2, continue from chapter 138 of the manga if you want to skip ahead.


Main Characters

  1. Yuji Itadori

    2. A kind-hearted teenager with immense physical strength who gets involved in the world of curses after a fateful encounter with a cursed object.

  2. Megumi Fushiguro
    3. Quiet and serious, Megumi comes from a well-known sorcerer clan. He deeply values saving people and has a mysterious family background.

  3. Nobara Kugisaki
    4. Bold and confident, Nobara fights curses while staying true to her own style and personality. She values friendship and self-respect above all.

  4. Satoru Gojo
    5. Charismatic and insanely powerful, Gojo is a teacher at Jujutsu High. He believes in changing the system for a better future and protects his students fiercely.

  5. Ryomen Sukuna
    6. An ancient, evil sorcerer reborn as a curse. Known as the “King of Curses,” he shares a body with Yuji, making him a dangerous wild card.

  6. Kento Nanami
    7. A former office worker turned sorcerer, Nanami is calm, logical, and mature. He takes his job seriously and acts as a mentor to younger sorcerers.

  7. Maki Zenin
    8. Born without cursed energy in a powerful clan, Maki fights using pure skill and cursed tools. She’s determined to prove her worth despite her family’s rejection.

  8. Yuta Okkotsu
    9. A special grade sorcerer with a tragic past. Quiet and reserved, Yuta holds immense power and is deeply connected to a curse tied to his emotions.

  9. Suguru Geto
    10. Once a friend of Gojo, Geto turns into a major enemy. He has a twisted vision of a world ruled only by sorcerers and is a key player in many dark events.

  10. Mahito
    11. A sadistic and unpredictable curse with a fascination for human emotions and suffering. He's one of the most dangerous enemies in the series.
